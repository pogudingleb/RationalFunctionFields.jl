## Scripts for running benchmarks

The instructions for running the benchmarks can be found in:

- `benchmark-1` directory: Benchmarking different implementations of Groebner bases over rational function fields. (Table 3 from the paper)

- `benchmark-2` directory: Computing small degree coefficients of Groebner bases over rational function fields. (Tables 1 and 2 from the paper).

- `benchmark-3` directory: Running our algorithm and the Maple one from (https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.07774) on our benchmark suite (Section 6 from the paper).
